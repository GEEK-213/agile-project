Tournament Rules & Guidelines

Player Eligibility

  • Age requirement: 16+
  • 5 players + 1 substitute
  • Verified Riot account
  • EMEA region only
  • No active VCT pros

Competition Format

  • 128 team double elimination
  • Bo1 until semi-finals
  • Bo3 semi-finals & finals
  • Match check-in: 30 mins prior

Prize Distribution

Champions - Per Player

  • $5,000 Cash Prize
  • Limited Edition Pro Jersey
  • OMEN Gaming Laptop
  • HyperX Peripheral Bundle

Runners-up - Per Player

  • $2,500 Cash Prize
  • Team Jersey
  • Gaming Peripherals Pack

Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet: 20+ Mbps
  • Working mic required
  • Anti-cheat verification
  • 60+ FPS capability

Broadcast Guidelines

  • 2-minute stream delay
  • TMN overlay mandatory
  • Stream quality: 720p+
  • Admin approval needed

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain sportsmanship
  • No toxic behavior
  • Follow admin decisions
  • Violations = DQ
